What do our sessions look like?

First session

whether you are a beginner or an advanced player we always start with a Day 0 session. During this youll get to meet the other players, get comfortable with the rules and design your charcters

Basic Campaign structure

Whether your playing on our bespoke online server or in person at one of our infamous live events, the games usually run over a period of 1 to 6 months, some people are still continuing their old campigns they started back in 1974! when the game was created.

Feel empowered

Every has had a rough few years following the closing down of the whole world along with the 9-5 daily struggles, taking time out of your week to become a fire weilding socercer wont fix everything, but its a start!

Get social

D&D players experience social support, practice collaboration, get to express themselves creatively. During the game they enjoy a brief and healthy escape from the stresses of daily life while also feeling as sense of control as they navigate the game
